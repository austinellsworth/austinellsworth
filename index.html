Welcome

Welcome to my website. This site is over 15 years in the making. Back in 2001 I was a freshman in high school. I had an assignment to pick a career and research it. I chose web development. As part of my project, I created a very simple website which I proudly showed to all of my classmates. They thought I was a wizard.

For one reason or another I did not keep up with web development, although I did continue to spend a lot of time working with computers and the internet. In 2017 I decided to get back into web development by taking a "Web Developer Bootcamp" course through udemy. After completing the front-end portion of the course, I realized that I now had the knowledge to build a simple website for myself without the aid of a program like WordPress. I was finally in a position to create a website, and felt I could make something worthy of being posted to austinellsworth.com, so I bought the domain.

This site is nothing more than a simple place for me to test out my HTML/CSS/JavaScript abilities and to post links to other things I am doing online. This site is mostly for my own enjoyment, but I hope that others will find it useful to them. If you want to see more of what I am writing, creating, and doing check back regularly!

About

I was born in Lansing, MI on April 18, 1987. I am the son of a retired MSU electrician and a State of Michigan employee. I graduated from Eaton Rapids High School in 2005, Spring Arbor University in 2009, and Anabaptist Mennonite Biblical Seminary in 2015. I married Allison Butler on July 11, 2009. She is presently a hospice chaplain. Together we have a beautiful daughter who was born on March 24, 2016.

As of September 2017 I am a student at AMBS once again to finish an MDiv. I am interested in ethics, social services, and technology. I'm not yet sure what type of career I will pursue after graduation, but I intend to develop theological/ethical, interpersonal, and web development/programming skills over the course of the next year. Ideally, I would combine all three into a profession.
